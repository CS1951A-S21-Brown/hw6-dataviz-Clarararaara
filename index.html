The dataset I picked is the video game data. After inspecting the data, I understand that the dataset had the name of games, platforms, publishers, and sales in different regions. For the first question I need to display the top 10 games of all time or a specific year. In order to address that I planned to make a bar plot of the top 10 games sorted by global sales and the sale amounts are shown in the x axis. I would like to display all time and in a selected range of the year. Therefore, I added a range slider to select given range of years that users want to see. As default, the top 10 games from 1980 to 2020 (all times) is shown. The video game may have the same name in different platforms, so I decide that I can add an interactive filter to filter the selection of platforms.
